When to use max-width property ?

Max-width is used in case of smaller section such as :- pop-up window, tool-tip

Diff b/w display :none and visibility : hidden

visibility : hidden , will keep element's css/area intact in the html and only hides the element.

display :none , will completely remove both element's area and hide it also.

Clear Property in css

Float and clear property goes in hand to hand. If we clear property then the text i.e the paragraph will come down.
